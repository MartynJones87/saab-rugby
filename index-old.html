With the untimely demise of the Saab brand, Ken has diversified into the rest of the family.

He now has full diagnostic equipment to cater for Vauxhall, Suzuki and Isuzu vehicles in addition to Saab.

Ken Jones has been trading in Rugby for over 20 years.

As Warwickshire's only Saab Specialist he offers a friendly, informal service which reaches high standards without the high costs.

It is these values which keep the old and new customers, reaching as far as the Lake District and Cornwall, returning.

Prior to working for himself, Ken gained 12 years of experience at a Saab dealership and garage in Leicestershire.

He is also a member of the Institute of the Motor Industry.
